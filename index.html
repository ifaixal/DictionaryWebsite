Sorrow

/ˈsɔɹoʊ/

noun

Meaning

  • Unhappiness, woe
  • (usually in plural) An instance or cause of unhappiness.

Parting is such sweet sorrow.

verb

Meaning

  • To feel or express grief.
  • To feel grief over; to mourn, regret.
